# Wahgola Product Vision
> AI-assisted travel planning with surgical precision and full visual control

**Tagline:** "Your trip. AI's help. Full control."

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Travel planning today is broken. People face two bad options:

### **Option 1: All-or-Nothing AI Planners**
**Examples:** Roam Around, Tripnotes, Layla AI

**The Problem:**
- Generate entire trip → If you don't like one part, regenerate everything
- No granular control over individual activities or days
- Can't easily tweak AI suggestions without losing good recommendations
- **User frustration:** "AI suggested great restaurants but terrible hotels - now I have to regenerate and lose the restaurants"

### **Option 2: 100% Manual Planners**
**Examples:** Wanderlog, TripIt, Google Maps

**The Problem:**
- Every restaurant, activity, and address must be manually entered
- No intelligent suggestions based on your preferences or context
- Time-consuming research (10+ hours for a 3-day trip)
- **User frustration:** "I spent my entire weekend planning instead of being excited about the trip"

### **Option 3: Hybrid Attempts (Poorly Executed)**
**Examples:** Tripnotes chat interface, Notion templates

**The Problem:**
- AI suggestions appear in chat/sidebar - hard to visualize in context
- No preview before applying changes (risky, trust-breaking)
- Can't selectively accept/reject individual edits
- **User frustration:** "AI changed my Day 2 but I only wanted help with Day 4"

---

## 💡 Our Solution: Granular AI Control + Visual Feedback

**Wahgola is the only travel planner that combines:**

### **1. Surgical AI Editing**
Edit individual days OR specific activities - not your entire trip
- User: "Make Day 3 more kid-friendly"
- AI analyzes ONLY Day 3 context
- Returns 3-5 specific changes (not regenerating everything)
- Changes merge into existing itinerary (preserves user's work)

### **2. Visual Preview Before Committing**
See every AI suggestion before accepting - with checkboxes to select which to apply
- Modal shows all proposed changes
- Each change explains AI reasoning ("Better for kids, interactive")
- Accept good suggestions, reject bad ones
- Apply selected changes in one action

### **3. Real-Time Map Synchronization**
Split-screen design: Map + itinerary always visible
- Click day → Map highlights all locations
- Click map marker → Scrolls to activity in itinerary
- GPS coordinates included in all AI suggestions
- Visual feedback on routing and distances

## 📝 Guiding Principles

### **Product Development:**
1. **User control always wins** - When in doubt, give users more control, not less
2. **Show, don't hide** - Make AI reasoning visible and understandable
3. **Mobile-first** - 70% of users browse on mobile, design for it
4. **Friction-free onboarding** - Guest mode first, auth later

### **AI Philosophy:**
1. **Suggest, never dictate** - AI proposes, user decides
2. **Preserve user work** - Never overwrite without explicit confirmation
3. **Explain reasoning** - Every AI change includes "why"
4. **Learn from feedback** - Track which suggestions users accept/reject

### **Business Model:**
1. **Freemium forever** - Free tier must be genuinely useful
2. **Value-based pricing** - Charge for outcomes (unlimited trips), not usage
3. **No dark patterns** - Transparent limits, clear upgrade prompts
4. **User trust = long-term growth** - Optimize for retention, not short-term revenue
